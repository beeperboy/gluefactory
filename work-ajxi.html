AJXI: Cosmic Climb

❮ ❯

Partnered with Nike to create "Cosmic Climb", a Game Boy Color game promoting the Air Jordan 11 Low sneakers. Integrated into the Nike SNKRs app, "Cosmic Climb" received over 500K plays and was showcased on billboards in NY and LA. The campaign led to the sneakers selling out in the first weekend, earning high praise from Nike for its creativity and impact.

Services: Campaign Development, Creative Direction, Graphic Design, Web Design, OOH, Game Development

Client: Nike

Year: May, 2024
